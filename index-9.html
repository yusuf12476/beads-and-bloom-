Behind the Brand

Rooted in tradition,
worn with pride

Beads & Bloom was born from a belief that beauty can be both ancient and alive. We work with a collective of Maasai and Kikuyu women artisans, paying fair wages and preserving techniques that have decorated East African women for centuries.

"A bead is not just a bead. It is a colour, a meaning, a memory."
