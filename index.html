Kakamega · Kenya

A craft born from community

Beads & Bloom was born from a belief that beauty can be both ancient and alive. Founded in Kakamega in 2018, we work with a collective of Maasai and Kikuyu women artisans — paying fair wages and preserving techniques that have decorated East African women for centuries.

"A bead is not just a bead. It is a colour, a meaning, a memory."

Every necklace, bracelet and pair of earrings we sell tells the story of the hands that made it. We believe in radical transparency: you can trace your piece back to the artisan who strung it, the market where the beads were sourced, and the community that benefited from your purchase.

Our pieces are not mass-produced. They are made slowly, with intention, one bead at a time.
